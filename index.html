How to Play

The player attempts to guess a five-letter English word in six turns. The player enters a valid five-letter English word into the input, then clicks the Enter button or presses Enter on the keyboard.

  • Green tiles show a guessed letter in the correct position
  • Yellow tiles indicate that the word contains the letter but in the wrong position
  • Gray tiles mean the letters are not in the word
Letters can repeat, and feedback applies accordingly.
